Frequently Asked Questions.

What is Truspace?

Truspace is a coworking space that provides serviced workspaces to individuals and businesses who need a place to work, create, study, start a business or run an existing one. From private offices to shared desk spaces to virtual office packages, we have a solution for everyone.
Check out our plans to find one that works for you. We have a fully equipped meeting room and functional kitchenette for your use as well and our training space is bookable for events at weekends.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 8am to 5pm, Monday to Friday, but closed at the weekends and most public holidays unless there is a scheduled event.

What plan works best for me?

Whether you need a workspace for a day or a month, for one person or for a team, we have a membership plan that fits your needs. Check out our plans to work out a solution that fits your needs.

What if I want to try Truspace out before committing to a plan?

You can purchase a 1-day pass to get a feel for our workspace. We know you’ll love it here.

What is coworking and does it work for me?

Coworking is a concept whereby you pay for daily, monthly or yearly access to a functional workspace shared with other individuals, either with similar interests or from diverse backgrounds depending on the vision and vibe of the space. It offers a convenient and cost-effective alternative to working from home for individuals, and to high running costs for start-ups and small businesses. Most coworking spaces offer a variety of workstations and flexible membership plans so you should be able to find a one that works for you from our plans.

Can I host an event at Truspace?

Yes, absolutely! Our meeting room and multipurpose training space are available to both members and non-members to host events. Learn more here or call us on 09030002705 to discuss the details.

How can I receive my guests or clients?

Guests are required to sign in and out at the front desk and to wear a visitor’s tag for the duration of their visit. Private Office users can receive their guests in their offices, while other membership types can attend to their guests either at the waiting area or in the meeting room (which you pay for or use the free meeting room access that comes with your plan).

Beyond just a physical space to work, our aim is to provide an enabling environment that fosters positive connections and empowers our members to succeed.
