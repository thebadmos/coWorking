Experience convenience and comfort .

Truspace is a serviced coworking space powered by Truckka Logistics, located in the heart of Maryland, Lagos.
We provide individuals and businesses with a clean, calm and comfortable space to do their best work, with easy access to business services and opportunities to connect and collaborate with other members of our growing community.

Enjoy flexibility .

We understand the need for business owners to keep costs lean and distractions low while maximising productivity and focusing on driving their business forward.

Our flexible and cost-effective plans offer the right amount of support and structure to help you and your business grow, with an array of services and amenities that will enhance your work experience.

Culture .

At Truspace we promote a culture of serious work, productivity and organic growth, aiming to foster a professional environment within which to work, welcome guests and facilitate positive business connections.

We believe where we work is as important as the work we do and here, we embrace the concept of workplace wellness by creating a warm, welcoming and clutter-free ambience that inspires and boosts creativity. We want you to not only love what you do but also love where you work.

Community .

At the heart of Truspace lies an entrepreneurial spirit which can be seen in the group of focused and driven entrepreneurs who make up the larger part of our community. We know that collaboration is the oil of progress and it important to us that our members are able to connect, cross-pollinate and collaborate to achieve mutual goals.

Our doors are open to founders, freelancers, creatives, professionals and everyone who needs an enabling environment to create, work, study, start a business or run an existing one.
